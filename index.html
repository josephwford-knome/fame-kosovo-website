The founding moment

It began at a school in Breznë, in the mountains of Dragash, where a bulletin board was split in two — one half for academic announcements, the other a tribute to the UÇK. Co-founders Joseph Ford and Behar Shala saw in that wall exactly what was at stake.

Kosovo's next generation must be as free in mind and capability as their parents were in territory.

The conviction behind FAME — from hunger to recognition.

The Child Success Passport · Patent-pending

A record that belongs to the child — and travels with them for life.

The Passport is a family-controlled, consent-governed record of each child's academic, health, and developmental milestones. Achievements become portable, verifiable credentials a family grants — and can revoke — at will.

  • Family-held & consent-governed — you decide who sees what, and you can take it back.
  • Verifiable credentials any school, employer, or border can trust — no phone call required.
  • Population insight without exposure — aggregate research from anonymized data, never an individual child's record.
